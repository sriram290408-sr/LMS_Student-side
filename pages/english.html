How to speak English very well?

To speak English very well, you need consistent practice, total immersion through media, focusing on phrases not just words, working on pronunciation and active listening, and building confidence by speaking daily, even to yourself, while not fearing mistakes as essential building blocks for fluency.

What are the 4 skills of English?

When we say that someone 'speaks' a language fluently, we usually mean that they have a high level in all four skills - listening, speaking, reading and writing.

What is A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2 in English?

A1, A2, B1, B2, C1, C2 are levels in the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR), classifying language proficiency from Beginner (A1) to Mastery (C2), with A being Basic, B being Independent, and C being Proficient users, commonly used for English levels
